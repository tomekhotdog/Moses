# AoC 2023 Day 3 — Gear Ratios

You are given an engine schematic: a grid of digits, symbols, and dots (`.`).

A *symbol* is any character that is not a digit and not a `.`.

## Part 1
Sum all numbers in the grid that are adjacent (horizontally, vertically, or
diagonally) to at least one symbol.

## Part 2
A *gear* is a `*` symbol that is adjacent to exactly two numbers. Its *gear
ratio* is the product of those two numbers. Sum the gear ratios of all gears.
