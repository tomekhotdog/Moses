# AoC 2024 Day 8: Resonant Collinearity

A grid contains antennas, each a letter or digit denoting a frequency. For every pair of antennas sharing a frequency, "antinodes" occur at the two points collinear with the pair where one antenna is twice as far as the other.

**Part 1:** Count unique antinode positions within the grid bounds.

**Part 2:** Antinodes occur at every grid point collinear with two same-frequency antennas (including the antenna positions themselves) — count unique positions.
