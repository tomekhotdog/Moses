# AoC 2024 Day 4: Ceres Search

Given a grid of letters, **Part 1** asks for the total number of times the word `XMAS` appears, in any of the 8 directions: horizontal, vertical, and both diagonals, reading either forwards or backwards.

**Part 2** asks for the number of `X-MAS` shapes: a 3×3 region centered on an `A` whose two diagonals each spell `MAS` or `SAM`, forming an X out of two `MAS` strings.
