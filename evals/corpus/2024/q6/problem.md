# AoC 2024 Day 6 — Guard Gallivant

A guard patrols a grid map (`^` = start facing up, `#` = obstacles). The guard moves forward, turning right 90° whenever a `#` is directly ahead, until it walks off the map.

**Part 1:** Count the distinct positions visited before the guard leaves the map.

**Part 2:** Count the positions where adding one new obstruction would trap the guard in a loop.
